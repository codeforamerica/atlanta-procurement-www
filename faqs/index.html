Where do I pick up a bid document?
Bid documents can be purchased in the DOP Plan Room between the hours of 8:15 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. The Plan Room is located at 55 Trinity Avenue; Suite 1790; Atlanta, Georgia. There are additional Plan Rooms located throughout the City. Please call 404.330.6069 for the locations and telephone numbers of these Plan Rooms. Bid documents may be downloaded from this website.
